Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various ingredients such as meat, cheese, lettuce, and salsa. This versatile dish can be customized to your liking and is perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (or your choice of protein)
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 8 taco shells (hard or soft)
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa

Steps

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water to the skillet. Stir well and let it simmer for 5 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  3. Warm the taco shells according to package instructions.
  4. Assemble the tacos by filling each shell with seasoned beef, lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, sour cream, and salsa.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
