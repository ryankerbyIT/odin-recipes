Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is made with layers of pasta, cheese, and a rich meat sauce. It's baked to perfection and makes for a hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz tomato sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
